The premier industry convention for escape room and immersive gaming professionals to learn, connect, and experience together.

Why Attend?

Knowledge, Connection, Immersion

RECON offers a curated collection of talks and experiences exploring the business and art of escape room and immersive game creation.

For everyone, The RECON community is a brilliant, social, and caring group of people who share a passion for making and playing games. Bringing everyone together creates opportunities for serendipity, both personal and professional.

On the business / creator side, the featured talks and workshops will explore the intersections of puzzle design, narrative, and technology, among many other topics.

For players, you’re getting a behind-the-scenes look at the artistry and technology that goes into games. Think of it as a view into the minds of the industry’s best creators.

Speaker Sessions

Industry experts present talks on select topics exploring the business and art of escape room creation.

Live Demos

Sponsors present their products and services for industry professionals and/or players, live on the demo stage in the RECON Discord.

Join the RECON Community

Hi there,

We believe RECON is better when more voices are in the room—whether you’re an escape room owner, designer, maker, enthusiast, or first-time player. And we believe that you being part of RECON makes it better for everyone.

That’s why we’re once again offering our Basic Access ticket for FREE, as part of our mission to make RECON Remote accessible to our global community.

But RECON is also a labor of love—brought to life by a small, passionate team, and made possible by those who choose to support it. If you’re in a position to do so, we invite you to purchase a Community Ticket or Pro Ticket. You’ll be directly supporting this event and helping us keep it sustainable for the future. Plus, we think you’ll love the extra access and connection it brings.

Whether you go free or paid, the most important thing is that you’re with us. If you do claim a Basic Ticket, we’d be so grateful if you could help spread the word—share a link, post on social, or invite a friend. Every little bit helps build this amazing community.

Thanks for being here. The future of immersive gaming is bright—and even brighter with you in it.

With gratitude,

Lisa, David, & the RECON Team
